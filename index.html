    The Shrine of St. Antony situated in ellakal, Kerala is one of the biggest pilgrim centres of St. Antony in the world; perhaps second only to Padua in terms of popularity.This Shrine belongs to the Archdiocese of Verapoly and is under the pastoral and administrative care of the Metropolitan Archbishop of Verapoly.

Holy Mass

Do You Know Jesus Christ From Nazareth?

The Holy Eucharist is the Sacrament in which Jesus Christ is really and physically present under the appearances of bread and wine. It is the summit of Christian Theology and belief. Every Holy Mass is the participation in the sacrifice of Jesus in Calvary. Everyday , Holy mass in Malayalam is offered in our shrine in morning and evening. Every Tuesday, there are 4 Holy Masses in which 7 pm mass will be in English. Every Sunday, there are 2 Malayalam masses and one English, Hindi, And Tamil masses each.
